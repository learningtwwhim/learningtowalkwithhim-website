I did not write this book alone.

That sounds like something authors say. In this case it is just true.

From the family members who encouraged me to keep going when this was still just a letter to my sister, to the artist who created a cover I could not be more grateful for, to every early reader who took the time to sit with these pages and tell me honestly what they thought, this book exists because of people who believed in it before it was a book.

I am grateful for every single one of you.

Today, *Learning to Walk with Him* is available. Paperback and eBook, at the link below. If you have been waiting, the wait is over.

If you have already ordered it, thank you. If you have not, I hope you will. And if you feel like it belongs in the hands of a missionary, a young person heading out to serve, or someone who needs a reminder that the Savior has not forgotten them, please pass it along.

Follow along on Instagram at [@learningtowalkwithhim](https://www.instagram.com/learningtowalkwithhim) and sign up for the newsletter at [learningtowalkwithhim.com](https://learningtowalkwithhim.com). When posts and reels go up, please share them. That is how this reaches the people who need it most. You never know whose hands it is supposed to land in.

For now, just thank you.
